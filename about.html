About US

The Passion Behind the Jersey

Soccer has been my greatest love for as long as I can remember. As a kid, I spent hours kicking a ball against the wall, pretending I was scoring the winning goal. My closet was filled with jerseys, each one holding a memory. But as life got busier, the game faded into the background.

That changed the day I found a vintage 1986 Argentina jersey in a thrift shop. Holding it, I felt something reignite inside me. That night, an idea took hold—what if I created a store where jerseys told stories? I poured my savings into a small downtown shop and called it "The Kit Stop." Soon, it became more than just a store; it was a place where fans gathered, shared stories, and relived great moments. One night, as I locked up, I smiled. I may not be on the field, but through "The Kit Stop," I’m still part of the game. And that’s all I ever wanted.
